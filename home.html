Location

Conveniently located in the heart of Raffles, our hotel serves as the perfect location to explore this city


Located at 20 Collyer Quay, Singapore 049319, our hotel offers a great location in the heart of Singapore, just steps away from the historic Raffles Place. Guests can reach the hotel via various different transportation options such as taxis, ride-hailing services, and public transportation like the nearby Raffles Place MRT station. The hotel has convenient access to a large number of nearby tourist attractions such as The Merlion, National Gallary Singapore, and Espanade.
